Pan Education Network Uganda (PEN-U) is an emerging organisation with ultimate desire for every child to access good quality and equitable education and learn.

About Us

Pan Education Network Uganda (PEN-U) is a non-denominational, child-focused education non-governmental organization. The organisation was founded in 2012 to support communities in jeopardy due to poverty to improve their welfare and the opportunity for their children to access equitable and quality education and learn. It was registered by the Uganda National NGO Board in under registration Number 9220.

Our Mission

To work in concert with all stakeholders to advocate equitable and good quality education for all Ugandan children irrespective of sex, location or socio-economic status so that they can be productive and progressive citizens in their respective societies and in this world that is more and more becoming a global village as well as a knowledge society.

Our Objectives

  1. To improve on enrolment, retention and learning achievement through research;
  2. To raise community awareness and involvement in education of the poor and vulnerable children;
  3. To train and/or retrain teachers to improve on their professional skills and to adapt to resource limited setting;
  4. To support in-school girls and boys to acquire learning skills;
  5. To support the provision of pre-school education of good quality to the rural and vulnerable communities;
  6. To provide out-of-school girls and boys with non-formal educations and/or job skills;
  7. To lobby for scholastic materials and facilities support for schools serving the poor and vulnerable communities;
  8. To promote a sense of responsibility among the in-school and out-of-school youth by providing them with accurate and age-appropriate information on sexual reproductive health and life skills;
  9. To support the participation of schools and communities in policy and actions aimed at caring for the environment and adapting to the eminent impacts of climate change;
  10. To coordinate and/or manage education sponsorship for the poor and vulnerable children;
  11. To provide professional services and consultancy.
