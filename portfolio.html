The Organisation Work Experience

Pan Education Network Uganda (PEN-U) is an emerging organisation with ultimate desire for every child to access good quality and equitable education and learn. With the generous support from World Vision, Tororo Programme Area and Plan International Uganda and South African Institute for Distance Education we have registered landmark achievements in our relevant fields as shown below.

May, 2016 to date
With support from South African Institute for Distance Education (Saide)
We support Early Grade Literacy improvement campaign in Korobudi and Pajwenda Primary Schools in Mulanda Sub-county, Tororo district through the following interventions:
  1. Empowering parents to take an active role in their children’s literacy development from well before the child starts school.
  2. Raising awareness of communities to work towards making reading a social practice in the language(s) of the community.
  3. Building teachers competence to understand not only how to teach reading in a sequenced way, but also provide sufficient reading practice and the opportunity of reading for enjoyment as well as learning.
  4. Providing sufficient local/familiar/home language reading material for young children to use for practice. Reality in schools shows that there is a drastic shortage of appropriate local African language stories for early literacy development for African children. Many of the books that do exist are expensive and difficult to distribute. They are usually funded for a while, and then go out of print, no longer accessible to the children for whom they were intended.
